FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I make a claim?

Contact our claims team via phone or email. We will advise on the required documentation and next steps, and assign a claims manager to your case.

Can I change my coverage later?

Yes. Most policies can be adjusted at renewal or by endorsement during the policy term; contact your adviser to request changes and to receive cost implications.

Do you provide corporate group plans?

Yes. We design and administer group health and life programs for organisations of various sizes and needs.
