INSURANCE GLOSSARY

Premium

The amount paid for an insurance policy, usually on a monthly or annual basis.

Excess / Deductible

The portion of a claim that the policyholder must pay before the insurer covers the rest.

Rider

An optional policy addition that provides extra benefits or alters the policy terms.

Endorsement

An amendment to an insurance policy which changes its terms, coverage, or scope.

Underwriting

The process an insurer uses to assess risk and determine the premium and coverage.

Claim

A formal request by a policyholder to the insurer for payment or coverage under a policy.
