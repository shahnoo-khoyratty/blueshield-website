FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Below you will find answers to the most common questions about our insurance products, services, and processes. Our goal is to provide clarity and help you make informed decisions with confidence.

How do I make a claim?

Claims can be initiated online, by phone, or through your advisor.

Click to expand / collapse
Once notified, our claims team will guide you through documentation, assessment, and settlement. Most claims are acknowledged within 24 hours.

Can I change my coverage later?

Policies can usually be adjusted as your needs evolve.

Click to expand / collapse
Changes may be made at renewal or via endorsement, subject to underwriting and policy terms. Your advisor can recommend suitable adjustments.

Do you offer corporate plans?

We design insurance solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Click to expand / collapse
Our corporate solutions include group life, health, liability, and employee benefit programs tailored to organizational needs.

How are premiums calculated?

Premiums are based on risk, coverage level, and personal factors.

Click to expand / collapse
Factors may include age, health, occupation, claims history, and selected benefits. Transparent pricing is a core principle at BlueShield.

What is not covered by my policy?

Every policy contains exclusions and limitations.

Click to expand / collapse
Exclusions vary by product and are clearly outlined in policy documents. We encourage reviewing them carefully with your advisor.

How do I contact support?

Our support team is available during business hours.

Click to expand / collapse
You may reach us via phone, email, or the contact form on our website. Dedicated account managers are available for corporate clients.
