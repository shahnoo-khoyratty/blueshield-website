LIFE INSURANCE

BlueShield offers a portfolio of life insurance products covering term, whole life, and hybrid solutions. Our approach is consultative: we assess cash-flow needs, liabilities, and long-term objectives before recommending policy architecture.

Products & Features

Term Life

Cost-effective cover for defined periods, ideal for income replacement and liabilities.
Click for more ...

Term Life provides affordable, high-limit protection for a chosen period. Ideal for replacing income, covering mortgages, or protecting dependents during working years. Simple structure, low premiums, no savings element.

Whole Life

Lifetime protection with savings element and potential cash value accumulation.
Click for more ...

Whole Life offers permanent lifelong coverage, guaranteed premiums, and the ability to build cash value over time. Often used for estate planning, wealth transfer, and long-term financial stability.

Why Choose Our Life Plans

  • Transparent terms and clearly defined exclusions.
  • Personalised benefit levels and riders.
  • Dedicated claims advocacy for beneficiaries.
