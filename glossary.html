INSURANCE GLOSSARY

Premium

The amount paid for an insurance policy, usually on a monthly or annual basis.
Click here to expand / collapse

Detailed info: Premium is calculated based on risk factors, policy type, and coverage level.

Excess / Deductible

The portion of a claim that the policyholder must pay before the insurer covers the rest.
Click here to expand / collapse

Detailed info: Deductible can vary by claim type, policy, and insurer; higher deductibles usually lower premium.

Rider

An optional policy addition that provides extra benefits or alters the policy terms.
Click here to expand / collapse

Example: Critical illness rider, accidental death rider, waiver of premium rider.
