SPECIAL COVERAGE PLANS

Click on a rectangle to see further details

Special coverage for high-value assets, critical illness, and expatriate exposures. These solutions are highly customised and are underwritten based on verified valuations or medical underwriting.

Critical Illness Cover

Lump-sum benefits to assist with treatment costs or income replacement following qualifying diagnoses.

Covers major illnesses such as cancer, heart attack, stroke, organ transplant, and other critical conditions. Provides financial support for treatment, rehabilitation, and income replacement for policyholders.

High-Value Asset Schedules

Scheduled valuations and tailored indemnity terms for collections, luxury homes and specialist equipment.

Includes jewellery, fine art, collectibles, luxury vehicles, and specialist equipment. Coverage is based on verified valuations with customised policy terms and limits.

These plans typically require bespoke underwriting and may include policy terms negotiated with specialist carriers.
