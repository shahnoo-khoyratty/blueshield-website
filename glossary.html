INSURANCE GLOSSARY

Premium

The amount paid for an insurance policy.

Premium is calculated based on risk factors and coverage.

Excess / Deductible

The portion paid by the policyholder before coverage applies.

Higher deductibles usually reduce premiums.

Rider

An optional addition to a policy.

Examples include critical illness or accident riders.

Endorsement

An amendment that changes policy terms.

Used to add or modify coverage.

Underwriting

The insurer’s risk assessment process.

Evaluates health, finances, and exposure.

Claim

A request for payment under a policy.

Requires documentation and insurer approval.
